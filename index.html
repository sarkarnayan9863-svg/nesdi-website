About Us

Building Tomorrow's Leaders Today

Our Mission

The North East Skills Development Institute (NESDI) is dedicated to bridging the gap between academic excellence and industry requirements. We provide comprehensive skill development programs and professional internships to empower the youth of North East India.

With a focus on practical learning and real-world experience, we prepare students to excel in their chosen careers and become valuable contributors to the workforce.
