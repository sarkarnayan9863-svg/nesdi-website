Our Vision & Main Objectives

Our Main Objectives

Swipe to see more

Skill Development & Capacity Building

Establish, operate and manage institutions for developing skill development, vocational education, digital literacy, skill upgradation and competency-based skill development, vocational education, digital literacy, skill upgradation and competency-based skill development and industry-oriented training for youth, women, farmers, artisans, professionals and communities.

Education & Training (Formal & Non-Formal)

To undertake educational activities including formal and non-formal education, professional education, coaching, mentoring, counselling, career guidance, digital literacy, e-learning, distance learning, blended learning and continuing education programmes.

Emerging & Future Technologies

To promote and provide training in emerging technologies including: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Analytics, Robotics, Internet of Things (IoT), Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, Digital Marketing, FinTech, Web 3.0, Metaverse, Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR) and Quantum Computing, etc.

Youth & Women Empowerment

To promote youth development, women empowerment, gender equality, leadership development, mentorship, awareness development, entrepreneurship and socio-economic upliftment, promoting livelihood and sustainable development.

Entrepreneurship & Business Development

To establish and manage incubation centres, innovation hubs and startup support systems to develop and manage innovative, business development and entrepreneurship management programmes for startups, MSMEs and livelihood generation.

Health & Wellness

To promote preventive healthcare, mental health, nutrition, wellness, telemedicine, and public health awareness, elderly care, rehabilitation and community healthcare programmes.

Agriculture & Allied Sectors Development

To undertake activities related to agriculture, organic farming, horticulture, sericulture, food processing, animal husbandry, fisheries, animal care, development and agri-entrepreneurship with value chain development.

Climate Action & Sustainability

To undertake programmes for environmental conservation, biodiversity protection, renewable energy, climate resilience, water conservation, waste management, carbon footprint management, carbon accounting, carbon credits, ESG programmes in carbon footprint management, supply chains, green buildings, clean technologies and net-zero transition strategies.

Culture, Heritage and Creative Industries

To preserve and promote indigenous knowledge, art, culture, heritage, handicrafts, eco-tourism, cultural tourism and creative industries.

Digital Transformation and Governance

To promote digital literacy, digital inclusion, e-governance, digital public infrastructure and technology-enabled services for citizens and communities.

Sports and Physical Education

To establish and support sports academies, fitness centres, adventure sports, and physical education and programmes promoting physical and mental well-being.

Consultancy and Advisory Services

To provide consultancy, advisory services, technical assistance, project management, monitoring and evaluation services to governments, public sector undertakings (PSUs), NGOs, CSR foundations, multinational agencies and international organizations.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Projects

To undertake, implement and manage Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) projects and programmes under Schedule VIII of the Companies Act, 2013, in collaboration with government departments, corporations, foundations and donor agencies.

International Cooperation and Development

To collaborate with national and international organizations, universities, institutions, agencies and foundations for the promotion of education, skill development, innovation and sustainable development.

Humanitarian and Relief Activities

To provide relief and rehabilitation during natural disasters, pandemics, emergencies and humanitarian crises, and to undertake charitable activities for the welfare of society.

Establish Institutions and Infrastructure

Establish, own, manage and operate schools, colleges, training centres, incubation centres, laboratories, hostels, workshops, libraries and digital platforms.

Organise Events and Publications

Organise workshops, seminars, conferences, exhibitions, competitions, festivals and awareness programmes. Develop curricula, publish educational materials, journals, e-learning content and digital platforms.

Communication & Soft Skills

To develop effective communication, public speaking, interpersonal skills, emotional intelligence, teamwork, time management, and professional etiquette to prepare youth for the modern workplace and leadership roles.

Media, Content Creation & Journalism

To provide training in digital content creation, video production, photography, podcasting, social media management, journalism, and storytelling — empowering individuals to build careers in the growing digital media industry.

Legal Awareness & Rights Education

To promote legal literacy, awareness of fundamental rights, consumer rights, women's rights, labour laws, and civic responsibilities among youth and communities to empower them to navigate legal systems confidently.

Our Leadership

Meet Our Directors

At the North East Skill Foundation, we are committed to transforming lives through education, innovation, and skill development. Our mission is to empower youth, women, and communities with future-ready skills, leadership, and entrepreneurial capabilities, fostering sustainable livelihoods and inclusive growth. Together, let us build a skilled, resilient, and prosperous North East that contributes confidently to India's progress.

Deepankar Bhattacharjya
Director

We believe every young person deserves the opportunity to build a successful future through the power of skills and education. Me as a globally certified Trainer and Facilitator and the founder of a Hospitality Management Training Institute, my vision is to empower the youth of Northeast India with industry-relevant skills, confidence, and leadership. Our mission is to transform potential into opportunity by creating pathways to employment, entrepreneurship, and lifelong learning. Through quality skill development and community-driven initiatives, We aspire to reduce unemployment, bridge the skill gap, and inspire a generation of self-reliant individuals who contribute meaningfully to society and nation-building through North East Skill Foundation (NESF).

Pine Bharali
Director
