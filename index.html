About Us

Our Vision & Main Objectives

Our Mission

The North East Skill Foundation (NESF) is a registered non-profit organization under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013, dedicated to holistic community development, education, skill enhancement, and inclusive growth across North East India.

Our Core Focus Areas

Education & Skills
Emerging Tech
Sustainability
Community Welfare

Our 10 Main Objectives

Educational Institutions

Establish & manage educational institutions, training centres, digital platforms for formal, non-formal, vocational & professional education.

Emerging Technologies

Promote education, research & innovation in AI, ML, Data Science, Robotics, IoT, Blockchain, Cybersecurity & other future technologies.

Incubation & Entrepreneurship

Establish incubation centres, innovation hubs & startup support systems to promote entrepreneurship, employment & livelihood generation.

Inclusive Development

Undertake youth & women empowerment, gender equality, rural/urban development, poverty alleviation & social justice initiatives.

Healthcare & Wellness

Promote preventive healthcare, nutrition, sanitation, mental health, telemedicine & community wellness programmes.

Environmental Conservation

Promote environmental conservation, renewable energy, waste management, sustainable agriculture & net-zero transition initiatives.

Research & Policy Advocacy

Undertake research, studies, policy advocacy, impact assessment & knowledge dissemination in key development sectors.

Culture & Heritage

Preserve & promote indigenous knowledge systems, art, culture, heritage, handicrafts, eco-tourism, sports & holistic development.

Humanitarian Relief

Undertake charitable, relief & rehabilitation programmes during disasters, pandemics & humanitarian crises.

CSR Projects

Undertake, support & manage CSR projects under Schedule VII of Companies Act, 2013 for public benefit.
